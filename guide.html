TypeMagic User Guide

Master the art of effortless text correction and formatting

Getting Started

TypeMagic is designed to be intuitive and powerful. Whether you're writing emails, posts, or documents, TypeMagic helps you communicate more effectively by automatically correcting errors while preserving your unique voice.

The Magic Shortcut

Press Cmd+Shift+M on Mac or Ctrl+Shift+M on Windows/Linux to instantly correct text anywhere on the web. This is the fastest and most seamless way to use TypeMagic.

Quick Start: Keyboard Shortcut

The keyboard shortcut is the recommended way to use TypeMagic for maximum productivity.

1
Click in any text field or select text you want to correct (email compose, comment box, text area, etc.)
2
Press the magic shortcut: Cmd+Shift+M on Mac or Ctrl+Shift+M on Windows/Linux
3
Wait a moment while TypeMagic processes your text (usually 1-3 seconds)
4
Your text is corrected instantly in place, ready to send or continue editing
Pro Tip: The keyboard shortcut (Cmd+Shift+M / Ctrl+Shift+M) uses the "Keep My Voice" tone by default, which fixes errors while preserving your writing style. For other tones, use the popup interface.

Tone Adjustment

TypeMagic offers three tone modes to match different contexts and audiences. Access these through the popup interface.

Keep My Voice

This is the default mode and is perfect for most situations. It corrects all spelling, grammar, and punctuation errors while preserving your unique voice, personality, and informal language.

  • Fixes all spelling errors
  • Fixes all grammar and punctuation errors
  • Preserves informal language like "gonna", "kinda", "lol"
  • Maintains your personality and word choice
  • Adds paragraph breaks if text is one long wall

More Professional

Use this mode when writing business emails, formal documents, or professional communications.

  • Fixes all errors
  • Replaces casual language with professional equivalents ("gonna" becomes "going to")
  • Removes slang and overly casual expressions
  • Maintains a respectful, business-appropriate tone
  • Preserves your core message

More Casual

Perfect for making formal text more conversational and approachable.

  • Fixes all errors
  • Makes formal language more conversational ("utilize" becomes "use")
  • Adds warmth and approachability
  • Keeps text natural and friendly
  • Preserves your original meaning

Convert to Bullets

The "Convert to Bullets" feature transforms paragraphs into clean, organized bullet points while fixing errors. This is perfect for:

  • Meeting notes and action items
  • Converting prose into presentations
  • Creating lists from stream-of-consciousness writing
  • Making information more scannable and digestible

To use this feature, paste your text into the popup textarea and click the "Convert to Bullets" button.

Google Docs Workflow

Due to browser security restrictions, Google Docs requires a copy-paste workflow:

1
Select and copy your text in Google Docs (Cmd+C or Ctrl+C)
2
Click the TypeMagic icon in your Chrome toolbar
3
Paste into the textarea at the top of the popup
4
Select your tone and click "Correct Text"
5
Paste back into Google Docs (Cmd+V or Ctrl+V)

Tips for Best Results

  • Text Length: TypeMagic works best with at least a few sentences. Very short text (1-2 words) may not benefit from AI correction.
  • Paragraph Structure: TypeMagic automatically preserves and improves paragraph structure. Write freely, even in one long paragraph, and let TypeMagic organize it.
  • Review Output: AI corrections are usually excellent but not always perfect. Always review before sending important communications.
  • Partial Corrections: Select only the text you want to correct before using the keyboard shortcut. Great for fixing specific sections.
  • Use Markdown: Enable "Use Markdown Formatting" in settings for enhanced formatting with headers, bold, italic, and lists.
  • Custom Prompts: Advanced users can create custom system prompts in settings to control exactly how TypeMagic corrects text.

Privacy & Performance

Privacy First

TypeMagic is designed with privacy in mind:

  • API keys are stored locally on your machine
  • No tracking, analytics, or data collection
  • Text is only sent to your chosen AI provider
  • Use Ollama for 100% local processing with complete privacy

Performance Tips

  • For fastest results, use gpt-4o-mini (OpenAI) or llama3.2 (Ollama)
  • Larger models provide better quality but take longer
  • Very long texts (5000+ words) may take 10-15 seconds
  • Check your AI provider's rate limits if corrections slow down

Troubleshooting

Keyboard Shortcut Not Working

  • Make sure you're clicking in a text field first
  • Reload the page after installing or updating the extension
  • Check that you're pressing the correct combination: Cmd+Shift+M on Mac or Ctrl+Shift+M on Windows/Linux

Paragraphs Disappearing

  • This should be fixed in v1.1.0+. Make sure you're on the latest version.
  • If using contentEditable fields (like Gmail compose), the extension now properly converts newlines to HTML breaks

Corrections Not Applying

  • Test your connection using the "Test Connection" button in the popup
  • Verify your API key is correct in settings
  • Check your AI provider's dashboard for rate limits or billing issues
  • For Ollama: Ensure Ollama is running with ollama serve

Tone Not Working as Expected

  • "Keep My Voice" should fix errors while maintaining your style
  • "More Professional" will change casual language to formal
  • "More Casual" will make formal text more conversational
  • Try using the "Custom System Prompt" feature for fine-tuned control

Advanced Features

Custom System Prompts

Power users can create custom instructions for the AI in the settings page. Examples:

  • "Only fix spelling, leave grammar unchanged"
  • "Use British English spelling"
  • "Add technical terminology for software development"
  • "Write in the style of [specific author or publication]"

Multiple AI Providers

TypeMagic supports multiple AI providers for flexibility:

  • OpenAI: Best quality, fast, reliable (recommended)
  • Google Gemini: Generous free tier
  • Anthropic Claude: Excellent for nuanced corrections
  • Ollama: 100% local, free, private
  • Custom FastAPI: For self-hosted solutions

Markdown Formatting

Enable this in settings to have TypeMagic format your text with Markdown syntax, perfect for platforms that support it (GitHub, Notion, Slack, etc.)
